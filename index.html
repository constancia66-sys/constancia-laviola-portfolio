About Me

Constancia Laviola - Experienced in international productions, streaming platforms, feature films, documentaries, advertising and live events. Based in Madrid with immediate availability for productions across Spain and Europe.

Trilingual: Spanish • English • Portuguese

Constancia Laviola, 1st Assistant Director and Field Producer with extensive experience in high-demand international productions.

Based in Madrid and available across Spain and Europe, she works fluently in Spanish, English and Portuguese, bringing strong value to local productions, international co-productions and projects involving Ibero-American talent.

With more than 20 years of experience in film, television, streaming platforms, documentaries, advertising and live events, she has worked on productions for HBO, Prime Video, FOX Films, major international brands and large-scale events such as the Rio 2016 Olympic Games.

As a 1st Assistant Director she specializes in connecting creative vision, production planning and logistical execution, ensuring efficient communication between departments and smooth operation on complex sets.

Projects such as Pacto Brutal (HBO), As Seguidoras (Prime Video), Woman on Top (FOX) and numerous international productions have strengthened her ability to lead teams under pressure, solve problems in real time and deliver results in highly demanding environments.

Alongside her production career, she develops original content as a writer, producer and director, including award-winning short films and feature projects currently in development.

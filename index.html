About Me

Constância Laviola 1st Assistant Director • Field Producer • Producer Based in Madrid with immediate availability for productions across Spain and Europe. Trilingual: Spanish • English • Portuguese Experienced in international productions, streaming platforms, feature films, documentaries, advertising and live events.

With more than 20 years of experience in film, television, streaming platforms, documentaries, advertising and live events, I have worked on productions for HBO, Prime Video, FOX Films, major international brands and large-scale events such as the Rio 2016 Olympic Games.

As a 1st Assistant Director, I specialize in coordinating complex productions, managing schedules, supervising large crews and ensuring seamless communication between creative and technical departments.

Projects such as Pacto Brutal (HBO), As Seguidoras (Prime Video), Woman on Top (FOX) and numerous international productions have strengthened my ability to lead teams under pressure, solve problems in real time and deliver results in highly demanding environments.

Alongside my production career, I also develop and produce original content as a writer and director, including internationally awarded short films and feature projects currently in development.

I bring strong organizational skills, leadership, attention to detail and a practical understanding of every stage of production, from development through delivery.
